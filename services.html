Services

Mediumship Palmistry Birth Charts Clarification Tarot Readings
Mediumship sessions can be done in individual or group settings. Readings are a way to connect with departed loved ones. Sessions can last from 1-3 hours. Groups of 3+ eligible for group pricing. A print of your palm will be made and analyzed with you in real time. We will discuss all major lines as well as minor aspects, and interpret how these affect your life. Birth Charts reveal our astrological breakdown. You will gain insights as to how you operate and why. All Birth Chart analysis includes a PDF copy of notes made during your session. Clarification services include ritual cleansing of spaces involving clarifying smoke of Sage and Palo Santos. Commonly known as Smudging. The remainder of the smudge stick will be given to you to continue the rituals on your own. Tarot and Oracle card readings are used to give insight and can be as in depth as you like. Your reading includes a PDF copy of notes made during your session as well as a photo of your spread.
